Central Visayas, Philippines

Me and my partner Rose went to chocolate hills in bohol.

Chocolate hills is one of the main attraction here in bohol because of it's unique geological formation. Where hundred of of rounded hills turns to brown in summer season creating a visually stunning place for tourist.

Aside from chocolate hills. My partner and I tried and had lunch on Loboc river cruise which give you a unique experience on having a buffet lunch while exploring the wide area of bohol country side.

Jasper

After overnight camping, we go to different lakes on our way to Banff those lake and falls are Athabasca falls, Bow Lake, and Lake Louise. We also spent a day in banff before going to Calgary.
